SOCIAL HEALTH

Social health is more than just the prevention of mental illness and social problems. Being socially healthy means increased degree of happiness including sense of belonging and concern for others.

    some types of social health

  1. public health
  2. family relationships
  3. peer relationships

    Signs of Being Socially Healthy Include

  1. Having assertive skills rather than passive or aggressive ones.
  2. Balancing your social and personal time.
  3. Being engaged with other people in the community.
  4. Adapting in social situations.
  5. To be yourself in all situations.
  6. Treating others with respect.

    How to Determine Social Health

  1. Does the individual spend time with friends?
  2. Does the individual have good self-esteem?
  3. How well can the individual set and maintain boundaries?
  4. Can the individual communicate effectively about difficult topics?
  5. Does the individual feel supported by other people?
  6. Does the individual feel respected by others in their life?

    ways to improve social health

  1. get enough sleep
  2. eat healthily
  3. get regular exercise
  4. maintain a normal weight
  5. drink less alcohol
  6. and don't smoke
